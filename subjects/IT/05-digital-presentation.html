Important Points

    Slide Transition:

     It is the visual motion when one slide changes to next during a presentation

     

    Animation:

    It allows to put the text and graphics, in motion within a slide.

     

    Speaker notes:

    These are guided text used by the presenter during a presentation.

     

    Tips to make an effective presentation:

    • Know your topic
    • Use Key phrases about your topic
    • Avoid too much text on each
