Important Points

  • Minerals are found in varied forms in nature, ranging from the hardest diamond to the softest talc.
  • Geologists define mineral as a “homogenous, natura lly occurring substance with a definable internal structure.”
  • The total volume of workable mineral deposits is an insignificant fraction i.e. one per cent of the earth’s crust.
  • In India coal occurs in rock series of two main geological ages, namely Gondwana, a little over 200 million years in age and in tertiary deposits which are only about 55 million years old.
